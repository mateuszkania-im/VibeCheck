VibeCheck Privacy Policy (English)

1. Scope

VibeCheck provides anonymous realtime voting rooms, optional participant reflections, AI summaries for closed sessions, and result sharing features.

2. Data we process

  • Anonymous device identifier (locally generated UUID).
  • Room metadata (name, code, type, timestamps).
  • Votes (value 1-5, timestamps, room reference).
  • Optional reflection text entered by participants.
  • Subscription/entitlement state for Premium features.
  • Technical diagnostics and error/performance telemetry (if enabled).

3. Data we do not collect

  • No account registration required.
  • No name, surname, or phone number required.
  • No contact list or GPS location collected.
  • No personal photos/files requested by default.

4. Processing purposes

  • Provide core app functionality (create/join room, voting, sync, AI summary).
  • Prevent abuse (for example duplicate votes from one device).
  • Apply plan rules (Free/Premium, summary quota, retention windows).
  • Maintain security, reliability, and diagnostics.

5. Processors and third parties

  • Supabase (database, realtime, edge functions).
  • OpenRouter (AI summary generation).
  • Google AdMob (ads for Free plan users).
  • RevenueCat (subscription and entitlement handling).
  • Datadog (monitoring and error telemetry, if enabled).

6. Data retention

  • Free plan: default retention up to 7 days.
  • Premium plan: default retention up to 30 days.
  • Premium hosts may extend retention by +30 days according to in-app limits.
  • Expired data is automatically removed by backend cleanup jobs.

7. User rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, rectification, deletion, or restriction. Contact us at contact.

8. Security

We use encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS), backend access controls, and anti-abuse controls. No internet service can guarantee absolute security.

9. Children

VibeCheck is not intended for children below the age allowed by local law without guardian supervision. We do not knowingly process children’s personal data.

10. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version and effective date are always published on this page.
