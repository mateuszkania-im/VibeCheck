## English

### 1. Scope
VibeCheck provides anonymous realtime voting rooms, optional participant reflections, AI summaries for closed sessions, and result sharing features.

### 2. Data We Process
- Anonymous device identifier (locally generated UUID)
- Room metadata (name, code, type, timestamps)
- Votes (value 1-5, timestamp, room reference)
- Optional reflections entered by participants
- Premium entitlement/subscription state
- Technical diagnostics and telemetry (if enabled)

### 3. Data We Do Not Collect
- No account registration required
- No name, surname, or phone number required
- No contact list or GPS location collected
- No personal photos/files requested by default

### 4. Purposes
- Provide core functionality (create/join room, vote, realtime sync, AI summary)
- Prevent abuse (for example duplicate voting from one device)
- Apply Free/Premium rules (retention, quotas)
- Maintain reliability, security, and diagnostics

### 5. Processors / External Services
- Supabase (database, realtime, edge functions)
- OpenRouter (AI summary generation)
- Google AdMob (ads for Free users)
- RevenueCat (subscription/entitlement handling)
- Datadog (monitoring and telemetry, if enabled)

### 6. Retention
- Free: up to 7 days
- Premium: up to 30 days
- Premium host may extend retention by +30 days according to in-app limits
- Expired data is automatically removed by backend cleanup jobs

### 7. User Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, rectification, deletion, or restriction of processed data. Contact: **mateusz@mcat.one**

### 8. Security
We use HTTPS/TLS, backend access controls, and anti-abuse controls. No online system can guarantee absolute security.

### 9. Children
VibeCheck is not intended for children below the age allowed by local law without guardian supervision. We do not knowingly process children’s personal data.

### 10. Changes
This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest effective date is always shown at the top.
