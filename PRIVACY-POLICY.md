# VibeCheck - Privacy Policy

Effective date: **April 8, 2026**  
Controller: **VibeCheck Team**  
Contact: **support@vibecheck.app**

## 1. Scope
VibeCheck provides anonymous realtime voting rooms, optional text reflections, and AI summaries generated after voting is closed by a host.

## 2. Data We Process
- Anonymous device identifier (locally generated UUID).
- Room data (name, code, type, create/close timestamps).
- Vote data (1-5 rating and timestamp).
- Optional reflection text provided by users.
- Technical telemetry and error diagnostics (when monitoring is enabled).
- Subscription and entitlement state (when Premium is enabled).

## 3. Data We Do Not Collect
- No account registration is required.
- No name, surname, or phone number is required.
- No contacts list or GPS location is collected.
- No personal photos/files are requested.

## 4. Processing Purposes
- Provide core app features (room join/create, voting, realtime sync, AI summary).
- Prevent abuse (for example, repeated voting from one device).
- Enforce Free/Premium limits and retention windows.
- Maintain security, reliability, and diagnostics.

## 5. Processors / External Services
- Supabase (database, realtime, edge functions)
- OpenRouter (AI summary generation)
- Google AdMob (ads in Free plan)
- RevenueCat (subscription entitlement handling)
- Datadog (monitoring/logging, if enabled)

## 6. Data Retention
- **Free:** session history retained up to 7 days.
- **Premium:** session history retained up to 30 days.
- Premium host can extend retention by +30 days according to in-app limits.
- Data is automatically removed by scheduled cleanup jobs after retention expiry.

## 7. User Rights
Depending on local law, you may request access, deletion, restriction, or objection for processed data, and you may contact your local supervisory authority.

## 8. Security
We use HTTPS/TLS, backend access controls, and anti-abuse controls. No online system can guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children
The app is not intended for children below the local legal age without guardian supervision. We do not knowingly process children’s personal data.

## 10. Changes
We may update this policy over time. The latest effective date will always be visible at the top of this page.
