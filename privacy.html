Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

VibeCheck is an anonymous live polling app. This policy explains what data may be processed when you use the app and how to contact us.

1. Data We Process

  • Room data: room name, generated room code, timestamps.
  • Voting data: anonymous vote values (1-5), room association, timestamps.
  • Optional reflections: short participant text notes shared in room context.
  • Technical events: basic diagnostics and error telemetry when enabled.
  • Subscription status: premium entitlement state through purchase provider tooling.

2. No Account Requirement

VibeCheck does not require end users to create an account to vote. Participation is designed to be anonymous within the room context.

3. Camera Permission

Camera access is requested only for QR room join scanning. Camera data is not uploaded as media by this feature.

4. In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed via Apple StoreKit and purchase infrastructure. We do not collect raw payment card details.

5. Data Retention

Room-related data is retained according to product retention settings. Current baseline retention is 30 days, with optional extension under product rules.

6. AI Summary Processing

When host requests AI summary, room statistics and reflections may be processed by backend AI integration to generate the summary text.

7. Contact

Questions or privacy requests: support@mcat.one

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page.
